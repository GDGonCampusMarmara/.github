# Welcome to GDG on Campus Marmara University! 🚀

### 🌟 About Us
We are the **official student community at Marmara University**, proudly **supported by Google Developers**. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical software industry requirements by using Google technologies and open-source collaboration.

---

### 🎯 Our Mission
* **Learn & Grow:** Hosting Study Jams and hands-on workshops .
* **Build for Good:** Participating in the Google Solution Challenge to solve local problems using global tech.
* **Community First:** Creating an inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds can collaborate on open-source projects.

---


### 🤝 Join Our Journey
Whether you are a beginner or an experienced developer, there is a place for you!
- **Contribute:** Check out our open-source projects and submit a PR!
- **Learn:** Join our Study Jams ,workshops and events.
- **Network:** Connect with fellow students and industry experts.
